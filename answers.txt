The disparity in school enrollment is significant: children from the poorest households are four times more likely to be out of school than those from the richest households.

Question: 9.  What are some of the persistent inequalities faced by women and girls, despite progress in areas like education and employment?
Answer: Despite progress in areas like education and employment, women and girls still face persistent inequalities in the following areas:

*   **Work and wages:** There are still gross inequalities in work and wages.
*   **Unpaid work:** A significant amount of unpaid "women's work" such as childcare and domestic work continues to be disproportionately carried out by women.
*   **Discrimination in decision-making:** Women face discrimination in public decision-making processes.
